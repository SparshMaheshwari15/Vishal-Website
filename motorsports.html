Facilities for Motorsports Manufacturing

  • Autoclave Excellence: Featuring a Scholz autoclave with a 5-meter length, 2-meter diameter, 16 vacuum ports, and a maximum pressure capacity of 10.5 Bar, our autoclave enables precise curing for high-performance composites.
  • Class 8 Cleanroom: A meticulously maintained environment, Class-8, 700+ sq. mtrs of area ideal for manufacturing & assembling sensitive aerospace components while adhering to strict contamination control standards.
  • Advanced CNC Machining: Our Breton CNC machine, 4-meter length & 2-meter width delivers unparalleled accuracy in tool machining, trimming and shaping parts, ensuring each product meets exact specifications.
  • Precision Scanning: Equipped with Faro 7-axis scanning technology, we conduct detailed measurements and quality checks to guarantee flawless components.
  • Freezer Storage: Dedicated storage solutions maintain material integrity and preserve the properties of advanced prepregs before processing, ensuring peak performance.
  • Surface Preparation Stations: Various surface preparation techniques as per the requirements of the customer.
  • Non-Destructive Testing: Non-destructive testing for evaluation of defects as per desired standard of testing.
  • Destructive Testing: Various destructive testing carried on test coupons using the universal testing machine for assessment of the main part.
